<p><p>Airway management devices are vital tools used to ensure proper ventilation during various medical procedures. The market includes different types such as endotracheal tubes, laryngeal mask airways, and others. Endotracheal tubes are inserted into the trachea to establish an airway, while laryngeal mask airways provide a secure airway without the need for intubation. Other devices may include supraglottic airways, oral airways, and nasopharyngeal airways. These devices are critical in maintaining airway patency and delivering oxygen to patients in need.</p></p>
