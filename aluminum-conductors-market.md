<p><p>The Aluminum Conductors market is divided into three main types: GJ, LGJ, and LGJF. GJ refers to Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced (ACSR) conductors, which are commonly used in overhead transmission lines. LGJ stands for Aluminum Conductor Aluminum Clad Steel Reinforced (ACSR/AW) conductors, which have a higher tensile strength and are suitable for longer spans. LGJF refers to Aluminum Conductor Steel Supported (ACSS) conductors, which offer improved conductivity and are ideal for high-temperature environments. Each type caters to specific needs in the electrical transmission and distribution sector.</p></p>
