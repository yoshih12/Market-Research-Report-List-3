<p><p>Anti-sway systems are used in various industries to prevent excessive swaying of equipment or machinery during operation. There are two main types of anti-sway systems: hydraulic and mechanical. Hydraulic anti-sway systems utilize hydraulic cylinders to counteract sway, while mechanical anti-sway systems utilize mechanical linkages or dampers. Both types of systems are designed to increase safety, improve productivity, and reduce the risk of accidents in industries such as construction, manufacturing, and transportation.</p></p>
