<p><p>Automotive Glass Fiber Reinforced Polypropylene (PP) is widely categorized into two types - long fiber and short fiber. Long fiber technology involves the use of long glass fibers which offer better strength and impact resistance compared to short fibers. On the other hand, short fiber technology utilizes shorter fibers which provide good surface finish and dimensional stability. Long fiber PP is preferred in applications where high mechanical properties are required, while short fiber PP is commonly used in parts where aesthetics and dimensional accuracy are crucial.</p></p>
