<p><p>Automotive interior coatings market can be categorized into two main types: water-based coatings and solvent-based coatings. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly and have low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them a popular choice for automotive interior applications. On the other hand, solvent-based coatings are known for their durability and resistance to chemicals and abrasion. While solvent-based coatings have traditionally been more commonly used, the shift towards more eco-friendly options like water-based coatings is gaining traction in the market.</p></p>
