<p><p>Automotive pillar parts refer to the vertical supports that connect the roof to the body of a vehicle. There are four main types of automotive pillars: A Pillar, B Pillar, C Pillar, and D Pillar. The A Pillar is located at the front of the vehicle, connecting the roof to the body near the windshield. The B Pillar is situated between the front and rear doors, while the C Pillar connects the roof to the body near the rear of the vehicle. The D Pillar is found at the back of the vehicle, connecting the roof to the body near the rear window. Each pillar plays a critical role in providing structural support and safety to the vehicle.</p></p>
