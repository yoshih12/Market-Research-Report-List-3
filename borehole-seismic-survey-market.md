<p><p>Borehole seismic survey market types include Offset Vertical Seismic Profile, which involves placing sensors at various distances from the borehole; Zero-Offset Vertical Seismic Profile, where sensors are placed directly above the borehole; and Deviated-well Vertical Seismic Profile, which utilizes sensors in a deviated well to gather seismic data. Other market types may include variations on these methods or new technologies for conducting seismic surveys within boreholes. Each type offers unique advantages and applications for subsurface imaging and analysis.</p></p>
