<p><p>Climbing robots are categorized into two main types: Wall Climbing Robots and Pole Climbing Robots. Wall climbing robots are designed to navigate vertical surfaces such as walls and buildings using suction or magnetic technology. These robots are commonly used for inspection, maintenance, and surveillance purposes in various industries. On the other hand, Pole Climbing Robots are specifically designed to climb poles and perform tasks like maintenance, cleaning, and monitoring in industries like telecommunications and power distribution. Both types of climbing robots offer efficient and safe solutions for accessing hard-to-reach areas.</p></p>
