<p><ul><li>Contrast Enhancement</li><li>Broadband Filters</li><li>Passband Filters</li><li>Customized Color Filters</li><li>Polarization Mirror</li><li>Band Rejection</li><li>Infrared Resistance Heat Reflector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Colour filters are used in various markets for different applications. Contrast enhancement filters are used to improve image clarity and definition. Broadband filters allow a wide range of wavelengths to pass through, while passband filters only allow specific wavelengths. Customized color filters are tailored to specific color requirements. Polarization mirrors enhance contrast and reduce glare. Band rejection filters block certain wavelengths. Infrared resistance heat reflectors are used to reflect heat while allowing visible light to pass through. Each type of filter serves a specific purpose in different markets.</p></p>
