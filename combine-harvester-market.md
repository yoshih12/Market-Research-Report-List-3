<p><p>Combine harvesters are agricultural machines used for harvesting crops such as wheat, corn, and soybeans. There are three main types of combine harvesters on the market: self-propelled combine harvesters, tractor-pulled combine harvesters, and Power Take Off (PTO)-Powered combine harvesters. Self-propelled combine harvesters have their own engine and are driven independently. Tractor-pulled combine harvesters are connected to a tractor for power. PTO-Powered combine harvesters are powered by the tractor's power take-off shaft. Each type offers different benefits and features for farmers depending on their needs and preferences.</p></p>
