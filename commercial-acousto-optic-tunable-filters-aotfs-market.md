<p><p>Commercial Acousto-Optic Tunable Filters (AOTFs) are devices used in various applications such as spectroscopy, telecommunications, and imaging. There are two main types of AOTFs in the market: Collinear AOTFs and Noncollinear AOTFs. Collinear AOTFs have the incoming and diffracted light traveling in the same direction, making them suitable for high-resolution applications. On the other hand, Noncollinear AOTFs have the incoming and diffracted light traveling in different directions, which allows for broader tuning ranges and faster tuning speeds.</p></p>
