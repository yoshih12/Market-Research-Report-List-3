<p><p>Construction equipment telematics can be divided into two main types: cellular and satellite market. Cellular telematics use mobile networks to transmit data from the equipment to a central system, allowing for real-time tracking and monitoring. On the other hand, satellite telematics utilize satellite signals to provide coverage in remote or off-grid areas where cellular networks are not available. Both types of telematics offer valuable data insights for construction companies, helping them improve efficiency, productivity, and safety on job sites.</p></p>
