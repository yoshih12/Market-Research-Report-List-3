All-In-One Type meters combine the flow sensor, transmitter, and other components into a single unit, offering a more compact and integrated solution. Separation Type meters have a separate sensor and transmitter, allowing for flexibility in installation and maintenance. Both types have their advantages and are suitable for different applications based on factors such as space constraints and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
