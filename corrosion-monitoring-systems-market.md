<p><strong>The Corrosion Monitoring Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrusive Corrosion Monitoring Systems</li><li>Non-intrusive Corrosion Monitoring Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrosion Monitoring Systems come in two main types - Intrusive and Non-intrusive. Intrusive systems involve physically inserting probes into the monitored equipment to directly measure corrosion rates. On the other hand, Non-intrusive systems use external methods such as sensors, ultrasonic devices, or electromagnetic technologies to monitor and analyze corrosion without the need for direct contact with the equipment. Each type has its advantages and applicability depending on the specific requirements of the monitoring process.</p></p>
