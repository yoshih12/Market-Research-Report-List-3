<p><p>Digital cleaning machines market can be classified into various types based on their working mechanisms. Hydraulic type machines use water pressure to clean surfaces effectively. Dynamic machines utilize mechanical vibrations to remove dirt and grime from different surfaces. Ultrasonic type machines use high-frequency sound waves to create cavitation bubbles that clean objects thoroughly. Other types of digital cleaning machines may include steam cleaners or pressure washers, each offering unique cleaning capabilities for various applications.</p></p>
