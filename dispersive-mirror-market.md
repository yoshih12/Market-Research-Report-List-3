<p><p>Dispersive mirror markets such as Gires-Tournois Interferometer (GTI) Mirrors and Chirped Mirrors are widely used in the field of optics and lasers. GTI Mirrors are commonly used for ultrafast laser applications due to their ability to provide precise dispersion compensation. Chirped Mirrors, on the other hand, have a varying reflective index across the surface, allowing for dispersion control over a broad spectral range. Both types of mirrors are essential for achieving high-quality optical performance in various applications such as pulse compression and frequency comb generation.</p></p>
