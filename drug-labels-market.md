<p><p>Drug labels can be categorized into different types based on their application method and design. Pressure-sensitive labels are self-adhesive labels that stick to the packaging when pressure is applied. Glue-applied labels require a separate adhesive to be applied to the label before attaching it to the packaging. Other types of drug labels include shrink sleeve labels, wrap-around labels, and booklet labels. Each type of label offers unique benefits and is chosen based on factors such as packaging material and design requirements.</p></p>
