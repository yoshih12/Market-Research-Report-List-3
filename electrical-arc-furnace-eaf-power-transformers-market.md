<p><strong>The Electrical Arc Furnace (EAF) Power Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct EAF</li><li>Indirect EAF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct EAF transformers are used in the direct arc furnace process where the transformer is directly connected to the electrodes that generate the arc. This type of transformer is designed to handle high currents and voltages required for the melting process. On the other hand, Indirect EAF transformers are used in the indirect arc furnace process where the transformer is connected to the transformer secondary circuit, which then supplies power to the electrodes. These transformers are more versatile and can be used in various types of arc furnace processes.</p></p>
