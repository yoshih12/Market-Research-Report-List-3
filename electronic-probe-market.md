<p><ul><li>Touch Probe</li><li>Non-Contact Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic probe market is segmented into two main types: touch probe and non-contact probe. Touch probes are designed to physically touch the surface to take measurements, offering high accuracy and reliability. Non-contact probes, on the other hand, use methods such as laser or optical technology to measure without physically touching the surface, allowing for faster and more delicate measurements. Both types of probes are widely used in various industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive for quality control and inspection purposes.</p></p>
