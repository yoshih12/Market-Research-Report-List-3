<p><p>Espresso coffee machines come in three main types in the market - manually operated, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manually operated machines require the user to control the entire brewing process, making it more hands-on. Semi-automatic machines offer some automated features, such as temperature control and pressure regulation, but still require some manual intervention. Fully automatic machines handle the entire brewing process from start to finish with just the press of a button, offering convenience and consistency. Each type caters to different preferences and levels of expertise in making espresso coffee.</p></p>
