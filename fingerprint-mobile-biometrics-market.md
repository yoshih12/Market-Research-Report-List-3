<p><p>The Fingerprint Mobile Biometrics market consists of two main types of technologies: Non-AFIS Technology and AFIS Technology. Non-AFIS Technology involves the use of fingerprint recognition technology that does not require a connection to a centralized database, making it suitable for mobile applications. In contrast, AFIS Technology utilizes Automated Fingerprint Identification Systems that compare and match fingerprints against a database of known prints, making it more suitable for law enforcement and large-scale identification purposes. Both technologies play a crucial role in the growing mobile biometrics market.</p></p>
