<p><p>Flatbed document scanners are classified into two main types based on the technology used for scanning: CCD scanners and CIS scanners. </p><p>CCD scanners use a series of charged-coupled devices to capture high-quality images with sharp detail and accurate color representation. These scanners are preferred for scanning documents with intricate details or images.</p><p>CIS scanners, on the other hand, use contact image sensors to capture images. These scanners are more compact and energy-efficient compared to CCD scanners, making them suitable for scanning documents quickly and in high volumes.</p></p>
