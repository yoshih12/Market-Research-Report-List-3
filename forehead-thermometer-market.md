<p><p>Forehead thermometers are available in two main types: non-contact and contact. Non-contact forehead thermometers use infrared technology to measure temperature without any physical contact with the person's skin, making them convenient and hygienic. Contact forehead thermometers require direct skin contact to measure temperature accurately. Both types of forehead thermometers are widely used in healthcare settings, households, and public spaces to quickly and accurately monitor body temperature for early detection of fever or illness.</p></p>
