<p><p>The Fruit Vegetable Enzyme Market includes various types of enzymes such as Pectinase, Amylase, Protease, and Cellulase. Pectinase enzymes break down pectin in fruits and vegetables, aiding in juice extraction. Amylase enzymes help in breaking down starches into sugars, improving flavor and texture. Protease enzymes assist in protein breakdown, enhancing digestion. Cellulase enzymes break down cellulose, improving the overall quality of fruits and vegetables. These enzymes play a crucial role in food processing, ensuring better taste, texture, and nutritional value.</p></p>
