<p><p>Hemodialysis Vascular Grafts are artificial conduits used for hemodialysis access in patients with kidney failure. They come in different types such as Polyester, Polytetrafluoroethylene, Polyurethane, and Biological Materials. Polyester grafts are durable, Polytetrafluoroethylene grafts are non-thrombogenic, Polyurethane grafts are flexible, and Biological Materials grafts are usually derived from human or animal tissue. Each type offers unique benefits for hemodialysis patients, depending on factors such as durability, biocompatibility, and ease of implantation.</p></p>
