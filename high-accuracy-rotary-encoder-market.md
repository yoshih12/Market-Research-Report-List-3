<p><ul><li>Incremental Rotary Encoders</li><li>Absolute Rotary Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High accuracy rotary encoder market can be divided into two main types: Incremental Rotary Encoders and Absolute Rotary Encoders. Incremental Rotary Encoders generate a series of pulses for each rotational movement, allowing for accurate speed and position measurements. Absolute Rotary Encoders provide a unique digital code for each angular position, ensuring precise positioning even after power loss. Both types are essential in various industries such as robotics, automation, and manufacturing for high precision applications.</p></p>
