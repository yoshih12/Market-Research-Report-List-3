<p><p>Hole transport materials (HTMs) play a crucial role in various solar cell technologies, including perovskite solar cells and dye-sensitized solar cells. Similarly, in dye-sensitized solar cells, HTMs help transport holes created by the absorption of light by the dye molecules.
