<p><ul><li>Frame Mounted End Suction Pump</li><li>Close Coupled End Suction Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal end suction pumps are classified into two main types: frame mounted and close coupled. Frame mounted end suction pumps have a separate baseplate and coupling to connect the pump and motor, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, close coupled end suction pumps have the pump and motor directly coupled together, resulting in a more compact design ideal for space-constrained installations. Both types offer high efficiency, reliability, and versatility for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
