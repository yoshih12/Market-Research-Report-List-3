<p><p>HVAC Control Systems consist of three main types of markets: Sensors, Controlled Devices, and Controllers. Sensors detect changes in temperature, humidity, and air quality, providing essential data for the system to operate efficiently. Controlled devices, such as dampers and valves, adjust the flow of air or water to regulate temperature and maintain comfort. Controllers act as the brains of the operation, receiving input from sensors and sending signals to controlled devices to ensure optimal performance of the HVAC system.</p></p>
