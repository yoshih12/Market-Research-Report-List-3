<p><p>Hydrochloric acid electrolysis market includes different types such as ODC electrolysis, Dupont Gas Phase Electrolysis, Diaphragm Electrolysis, and Sumitomo Process. ODC electrolysis involves the direct electrolysis of hydrochloric acid to produce chlorine and hydrogen gases. Dupont Gas Phase Electrolysis utilizes a gas-phase cell to separate chlorine and hydrogen. Diaphragm Electrolysis uses a diaphragm to prevent mixing of chlorine and hydrogen gases. The Sumitomo Process involves the oxidation of chlorine gas to produce hydrochloric acid, which is then electrolyzed to separate chlorine and hydrogen.</p></p>
