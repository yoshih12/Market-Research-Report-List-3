<p><p>Intracorporeal lithotripsy market includes various types of techniques used for breaking down kidney stones within the body. Mechanical lithotripsy involves using a mechanical device to crush the stones. Electrohydraulic lithotripsy uses electrical sparks to break the stones. Laser lithotripsy involves using laser energy to fragment the stones. Ultrasonic lithotripsy uses high-frequency sound waves to break down the stones. Other techniques include pneumatic lithotripsy and ballistic lithotripsy. Each type offers unique benefits and may be recommended based on the size and location of the stones.</p></p>
