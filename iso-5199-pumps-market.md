<p><p>ISO 5199 Pumps Market includes two main types: Magdrive Pumps and Sealed Pumps. Magdrive Pumps utilize a magnetic coupling to transfer torque from the motor to the pump, eliminating the need for a mechanical seal. This type of pump is ideal for handling corrosive or toxic fluids. On the other hand, Sealed Pumps rely on traditional seals to prevent leakage and are commonly used for less hazardous applications. Both types of pumps adhere to ISO standards for their design and performance.</p></p>
