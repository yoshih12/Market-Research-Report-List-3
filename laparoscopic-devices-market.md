<p><p>The laparoscopic devices market consists of three main types: laparoscopes, insufflators, and accessories. Insufflators are devices used to inflate the abdominal cavity with carbon dioxide gas, creating space for the laparoscope and surgical instruments to maneuver. Accessories such as trocars, graspers, and dissectors are used alongside laparoscopes to perform specific tasks during surgery. This market is driven by the increasing adoption of minimally invasive procedures for faster recovery and reduced scarring.</p></p>
