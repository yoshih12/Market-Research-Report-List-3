<p><ul><li>Plano-convex Lens</li><li>Meniscus Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser focusing lenses are widely used in various industries for focusing and shaping laser beams. Plano-convex lenses have one flat and one outwardly curved surface, which is ideal for collimating light beams. Meniscus lenses have one convex and one concave surface, providing a shorter focal length and minimizing spherical aberrations. The Plano-convex lens market caters to applications requiring collimated beams, while the Meniscus lens market is preferred for applications where a shorter focal length and reduced aberrations are necessary.</p></p>
