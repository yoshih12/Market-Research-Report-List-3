<p><p>Latex polymers are divided into two main types: Natural Latex Polymer and Synthetic Latex Polymer. Natural Latex Polymers are derived from the sap of rubber trees and are known for their elasticity and biodegradability. On the other hand, Synthetic Latex Polymers are artificially produced through chemical processes and are designed to have specific properties for various industrial applications. Both types of latex polymers are widely used in the production of various products such as adhesives, coatings, textiles, and medical devices.</p></p>
