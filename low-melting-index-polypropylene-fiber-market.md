<p><p>Low Melting Index Polypropylene Fiber market types are classified based on the melt flow rate (MFR) expressed in g/10 min. The categories are as follows: 0-1 g/10 min, 1.1-3.5 g/10 min, and 3.6-5 g/10 min. The lower the melt flow rate, the higher the molecular weight of the polypropylene fiber, resulting in improved strength and durability. Higher melt flow rates, on the other hand, are associated with lower molecular weights and are often used in applications where flexibility and ease of processing are prioritized.</p></p>
