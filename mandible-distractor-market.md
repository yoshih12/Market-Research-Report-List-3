<p><ul><li>Muti-vector Distraction</li><li>Bi-directional Distraction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mandible distractor market consists of two main types: multi-vector distraction and bi-directional distraction devices. Multi-vector distraction devices allow for movement in multiple directions, providing greater flexibility in the adjustment of the mandible position. Bi-directional distraction devices only allow movement in two specific directions, usually forward and backward. Both types offer surgical professionals the ability to gradually separate the mandible bones for correction or reconstruction, with each type having its own advantages and applications in various surgical procedures.</p></p>
