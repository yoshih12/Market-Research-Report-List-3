<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Static</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical compression stocking market can be divided into dynamic and static types based on their design and function. Dynamic compression stockings provide graduated compression that decreases from the ankle towards the thigh, promoting blood flow and reducing the risk of blood clots. On the other hand, static compression stockings provide consistent compression throughout the garment, helping to manage symptoms such as swelling and varicose veins. Both types play a crucial role in managing various medical conditions related to circulation and vascular health.</p></p>
