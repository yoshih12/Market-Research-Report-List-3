<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical laminated tubes are used for packaging pharmaceuticals, ointments, creams, and other medicinal products. There are two main types of laminated tubes in the market: ABL (aluminum barrier laminated) tubes and PBL (plastic barrier laminated) tubes. ABL tubes are made of a layer of aluminum sandwiched between plastic layers, providing excellent barrier properties against light, oxygen, and moisture. PBL tubes, on the other hand, are made of multiple layers of plastic material, offering good flexibility and printability. Both types are widely used in the medical industry for their superior protective and aesthetic characteristics.</p></p>
