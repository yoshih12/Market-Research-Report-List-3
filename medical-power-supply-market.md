<p><p>Medical power supply market can be categorized into two main types based on their functionality - AC/DC power supply and DC/DC power supply. AC/DC power supply converts alternating current (AC) from the mains electricity into direct current (DC) used to power medical devices. On the other hand, DC/DC power supply converts one DC voltage level to another, ensuring proper power distribution within medical equipment. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and reliable operation of medical devices in healthcare settings.</p></p>
