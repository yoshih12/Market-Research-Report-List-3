<p><p>Medical tourniquets are devices used to control bleeding by constricting blood flow to a limb. There are two main types of tourniquets: Latex Tourniquets and TPE Tourniquets. Latex tourniquets are made from natural rubber and are known for their durability and elasticity. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) Tourniquets are made from a synthetic rubber-like material that offers similar elasticity and strength as latex but without the risk of latex allergies. Both types are commonly used in medical settings for procedures such as blood draws and IV insertions.</p></p>
