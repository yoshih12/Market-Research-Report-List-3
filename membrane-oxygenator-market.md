<p><p>Membrane oxygenators are devices used in medical procedures to oxygenate and remove carbon dioxide from blood during surgeries or in patients with respiratory failure. Two main types of membrane oxygenators are flat sheet membrane oxygenators and hollow fiber membrane oxygenators. Flat sheet membrane oxygenators consist of thin sheets of membrane stacked together, while hollow fiber membrane oxygenators have a network of hollow fibers that blood flows through. Both types serve the same purpose of oxygenation and carbon dioxide removal, but differ in their design and efficiency.</p></p>
