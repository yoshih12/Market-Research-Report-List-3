<p><ul><li>Pulse Induction</li><li>Very Low Frequency</li><li>Beat-frequency Oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal detectors for frozen food market come in three main types: Pulse Induction, Very Low Frequency, and Beat-frequency Oscillation. Pulse Induction detectors are known for their ability to ignore mineral content in the food product, making them ideal for frozen foods. Very Low Frequency detectors are sensitive enough to detect small metal contaminants, while Beat-frequency Oscillation detectors are highly selective and can differentiate between different metals. Each type caters to specific needs in the frozen food industry, ensuring food safety and quality.</p></p>
