<p><p>Metal fabrication robots can be categorized into different types based on their design and functionality. Articulated robots are versatile and have multiple joints allowing for a wide range of motion. Cartesian robots move in a linear fashion along three axes, making them ideal for precision cutting and welding tasks. SCARA robots have a circular motion and are perfect for tasks that require speed and accuracy. Other market types include delta robots and collaborative robots, each offering unique benefits for specific metal fabrication processes.</p></p>
