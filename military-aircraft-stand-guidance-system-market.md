<p><p>The Military Aircraft Stand Guidance System market includes two main types: Visual Docking Guidance System (VDGS) and Advanced Visual Docking Guidance System (A-VDGS). VDGS provides visual guidance to pilots during aircraft docking procedures, helping them align the aircraft accurately with the stand. A-VDGS takes this a step further with advanced technologies such as sensors and cameras to enhance precision and safety during the docking process. Both systems play a crucial role in streamlining operations and increasing efficiency at military air bases.</p></p>
