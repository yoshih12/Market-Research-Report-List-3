<p><p>Military Electronic Chart Display and Information System (ECDIS) market includes two main types of electronic navigational charts (ENC) and raster navigational charts (RNC). ENC are vector charts that contain all the necessary information for navigation, while RNC are scanned images of traditional paper charts. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing situational awareness and ensuring safe navigation for military vessels. The market for these charts continues to grow as newer technologies and advancements are made in the field of marine navigation.</p></p>
