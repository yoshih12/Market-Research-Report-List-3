<p><ul><li>Internal Mold Releases</li><li>External Mold Releases</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mold release coatings are divided into two main types: internal and external releases. Internal mold releases are additives that are incorporated directly into the molding material to prevent adhesion between the material and the mold surface. External mold releases are coatings applied to the mold surface before molding to facilitate easy release of the finished product. Each type has its own unique application and benefits in the manufacturing process, offering solutions to different challenges in molding operations.</p></p>
