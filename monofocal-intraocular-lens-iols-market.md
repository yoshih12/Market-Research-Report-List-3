<p><p>Monofocal Intraocular Lens (IOLs) Market includes two types: Not Pre-loaded IOLs and Pre-loaded IOLs. Not Pre-loaded IOLs require the surgeon to manually load the lens into the injector before implantation. On the other hand, Pre-loaded IOLs come pre-loaded in the injector, saving time and reducing the risk of contamination during surgery. Both types of IOLs offer excellent visual outcomes for patients undergoing cataract surgery, with Not Pre-loaded IOLs requiring an additional step of manual loading by the surgeon.</p></p>
