<p><p>Non-Contact Radar Level Transmitter market includes two types: Guided-Wave Radar Level Transmitter and Non-Contact Wave Radar Level Transmitter. Guided-Wave Radar Level Transmitters use a probe to measure levels in liquids and solids, making them suitable for challenging applications. Non-Contact Wave Radar Level Transmitters, on the other hand, use radar pulses to measure levels without physically touching the substance, offering easy installation and maintenance. Both types offer accurate and reliable level measurement solutions for various industries.</p></p>
