<p><p>Optical Thickness Measurement System Market Types include Contact and Non-Contact systems. Contact systems involve direct physical contact between the sensor and the object being measured, providing accurate and precise measurements but potentially causing damage to delicate surfaces. Non-Contact systems, on the other hand, use optical technologies such as lasers or cameras to measure thickness without touching the object, making them suitable for fragile materials and high-speed measurement applications. Both types offer unique advantages and are used in various industries for quality control and process optimization.</p></p>
