<p><p>Organic Filtering Membrane Market includes three major types - Microfiltration, Ultrafiltration, and Nanofiltration. Microfiltration involves the removal of bacteria, suspended solids, and large molecules from water. Ultrafiltration filters out smaller particles including viruses and proteins. Nanofiltration is capable of removing even smaller particles like dissolved salts and organic compounds. These membranes are used in various industries such as water treatment, food and beverage processing, pharmaceuticals, and more, making them essential in ensuring clean and purified liquids.</p></p>
