<p><p>Organic linen market types include dry spun yarn, wet spun yarn, and blended yarn. Dry spun yarn is made using a mechanical process to create elongated, smooth fibers. Wet spun yarn involves soaking flax fibers in water to separate and spin them into yarn. Blended yarn combines linen with other fibers, such as cotton or wool, to create unique textures and properties. Each market type caters to different preferences for texture, durability, and overall performance in various industries such as fashion, home textiles, and crafts.</p></p>
