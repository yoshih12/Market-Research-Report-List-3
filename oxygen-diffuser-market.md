<p><p>Ceramic Fine Pore Oxygen Diffusers are a type of oxygen diffuser that utilizes small pores in a ceramic material to release oxygen into water. This design allows for fine bubbles to be released, increasing the surface area of oxygen diffusion. Cylinder Ceramic Oxygen Diffusers, on the other hand, are shaped like cylinders and also use ceramic material to release oxygen into water. Both types of diffusers are popular in aquaculture and wastewater treatment applications due to their efficient oxygen transfer capabilities.</p></p>
