<p><p>Paint spraying equipment market can be categorized into several types. Conventional sprayers are the traditional spray guns that use compressed air to atomize paint. High volume low pressure sprayers use less air pressure but more volume of paint to reduce overspray. Airless sprayers pump paint at high pressure for a smooth, even finish. Other types of spray equipment include electrostatic sprayers and air-assisted airless sprayers. Each type offers unique benefits for different applications in the painting industry.</p></p>
