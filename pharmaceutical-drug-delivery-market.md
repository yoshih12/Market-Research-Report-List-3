<p><p>Pharmaceutical drug delivery market types include oral, pulmonary, injectable, and topical methods. Oral drug delivery involves administering drugs through the mouth, commonly in pill or liquid form. Pulmonary delivery delivers drugs directly to the lungs through inhalation. Injectable delivery involves administering drugs through injections, either intramuscularly or intravenously. Topical delivery is the application of drugs directly to the skin or mucous membranes. Each method offers different benefits in terms of efficacy, convenience, and targeted delivery of medications.</p></p>
