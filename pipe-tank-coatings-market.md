<p><p>Pipe-tank coatings are protective layers applied to pipes and tanks to prevent corrosion and extend their lifespan. There are two main types of coatings on the market: powder coatings and liquid coatings. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and then heated to form a protective layer. Liquid coatings are applied as a liquid and then dry to form a solid protective coating. Both types offer excellent corrosion resistance and durability, but the choice between the two depends on factors such as application method and desired finish.</p></p>
