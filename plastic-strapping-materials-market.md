Polyester strapping is known for its high tensile strength and durability, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. Composite strapping combines polyester fibers with adhesive for superior strength and resistance to moisture. Polypropylene strapping is lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for light to medium-duty strapping needs. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in various industries for securing and packaging goods during transportation and storage.</p></p>
