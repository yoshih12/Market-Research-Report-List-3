Semiconductor leak detectors use a semiconductor sensor to detect refrigerant leaks by monitoring changes in conductivity when the gas is present. Both types of detectors are commonly used in the market due to their portability and efficiency in detecting leaks, making them essential tools for maintenance professionals in the HVAC and refrigeration industry.</p></p>
