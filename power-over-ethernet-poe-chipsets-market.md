<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Power-over-Ethernet (PoE) Chipsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PoE Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Chipset</li><li>PoE Powered Devices (PD) Chipset</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power-over-Ethernet (PoE) chipsets serve two main functions in the market. PoE Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) chipsets are used in devices that supply power to connected PoE Powered Devices (PD) chipsets over Ethernet cables. PSE chipsets are typically found in switches, injectors, and midspans. On the other hand, PD chipsets are integrated into devices such as IP cameras, VoIP phones, and access points to receive power and data through the same Ethernet connection. Both types of chipsets play a crucial role in delivering power efficiently and cost-effectively in PoE systems.</p></p>
