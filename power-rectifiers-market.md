<p><p>Power rectifiers are electronic devices used to convert alternating current (AC) into direct current (DC). There are two main types of power rectifiers on the market: Diode Rectifiers and Thyristor Rectifiers. Diode rectifiers consist of diodes that allow current to flow in only one direction, while thyristor rectifiers use thyristors to control the flow of current. Diode rectifiers are more commonly used for low power applications, while thyristor rectifiers are used for high-power industrial applications. Both types of rectifiers are essential in converting AC power to DC power for various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
