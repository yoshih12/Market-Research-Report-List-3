<p><p>Pre-loaded Intraocular Lens (IOLs) market includes different types based on their functionality and design. Monofocal IOLs are designed to correct vision at one distance, while Multifocal IOLs allow for multiple focal points to improve vision at different distances. Toric IOLs are used to correct astigmatism, resulting in clearer vision. Other types of pre-loaded IOLs may include accommodative IOLs, which adjust to different focal lengths, or extended depth of focus IOLs, which provide a greater range of vision. Each type caters to specific vision correction needs.</p></p>
