<p><ul><li>Automated Guided Vehicles (AGV) Systems</li><li>Monorail Systems</li><li>Powered Roller Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Programmable conveyors have various types in the market. Automated Guided Vehicles (AGV) Systems use vehicles guided by sensors or magnets to transport items within a facility. Monorail Systems utilize a single track for transporting goods in a straight line or along a predetermined path. Powered Roller Systems consist of motorized rollers that propel items along the conveyor. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of efficiency, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness in material handling and transportation applications.</p></p>
