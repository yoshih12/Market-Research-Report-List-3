<p><strong>The Readout Integrated Circuits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Readout Integrated Circuits</li><li>Analog Readout Integrated Circuits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Readout Integrated Circuits (ROIC) are used in imaging systems to convert signals from sensors into a digital or analog format. Digital ROICs process signals into binary data suitable for digital systems, while analog ROICs convert signals into continuous voltage levels suitable for analog systems. Digital ROICs are preferred for their high speed, low noise, and compatibility with digital processing systems. Analog ROICs are favored for their simplicity, low power consumption, and suitability for certain types of sensors that output analog signals.</p></p>
