<p><p>Regional oximeter market can be segmented into two types based on sensor usage: reusable sensor and disposable sensor. Reusable sensors can be used multiple times on different patients after proper disinfection and calibration, making them cost-effective in the long run. On the other hand, disposable sensors are designed for single-use only, eliminating the need for cleaning and calibration but potentially increasing overall costs for healthcare facilities. Both types cater to different needs and preferences within the healthcare industry.</p></p>
