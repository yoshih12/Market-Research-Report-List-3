<p><ul><li>Non-reactive Type</li><li>Reactive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resin diluent and modifier market can be categorized into non-reactive type and reactive type. Non-reactive type diluents and modifiers do not chemically interact with the resin, but simply act as fillers or extenders to improve properties like viscosity and durability. On the other hand, reactive types chemically bond with the resin, enhancing properties like adhesion and flexibility. Both types play a significant role in enhancing the performance and characteristics of resins used in various industries such as coatings, adhesives, and composites.</p></p>
