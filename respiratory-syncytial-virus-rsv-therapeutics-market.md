<p><p>Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV) Therapeutics Market comprises two main types of treatments: Palivizumab and Ribavirin. Palivizumab is a monoclonal antibody that helps prevent RSV infection in high-risk infants. It is typically given as a monthly injection during RSV season. Ribavirin is an antiviral medication that is used to treat severe RSV infection in hospitalized patients. It is administered through inhalation or intravenously. Both of these treatments aim to reduce the severity of RSV symptoms and prevent complications associated with the virus.</p></p>
