<p><p>Road Traffic Simulation Software has two main market types: Micro-level Traffic Simulation and Macro-level Traffic Simulation. </p><p>Micro-level Traffic Simulation focuses on simulating individual vehicles and their interactions in a detailed manner, often used for analyzing specific intersections or road segments. </p><p>Macro-level Traffic Simulation, on the other hand, deals with simulating traffic flow at a broader level, encompassing entire road networks and cities. This type of simulation is used for urban planning, transportation management, and policy-making decisions. Both types play a crucial role in improving traffic efficiency and safety.</p></p>
