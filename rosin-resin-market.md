<p><p>Rosin resin is classified into three main types: gum resin, wood resin, and tall oil resin. Gum resin is derived from living trees through tapping, while wood resin is obtained from dead trees and stumps. Tall oil resin is a byproduct of the paper pulping process. Each type of rosin resin has unique properties and applications in industries such as adhesives, coatings, rubber, and printing inks. The market for rosin resin products continues to grow due to their versatility and eco-friendly nature.</p></p>
