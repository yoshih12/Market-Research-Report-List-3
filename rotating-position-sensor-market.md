<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotating Position Sensor market types can be categorized into Contact and Non-Contact sensors. Contact sensors physically touch the object they are measuring, providing accurate readings but may experience wear over time. Non-Contact sensors, on the other hand, do not require physical contact with the object and use techniques like Hall Effect or optical sensors to measure position. While Non-Contact sensors offer benefits like longer lifespan and reduced maintenance, Contact sensors are preferred for high precision applications.</p></p>
