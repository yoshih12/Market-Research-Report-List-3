<p><ul><li>CTS (Conversion Track System)</li><li>TTS (Trailed Track System)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber tracks for agricultural machinery come in two main types - CTS (Conversion Track System) and TTS (Trailed Track System). CTS involves converting existing tires into tracks by replacing them with rubber tracks, providing better traction and reduced ground compaction. TTS is a separate track system that is attached to the machinery, allowing for easy removal and installation. Both systems offer benefits such as improved efficiency, traction, and stability, making them popular choices for agricultural equipment.</p></p>
