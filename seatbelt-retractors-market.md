<p><p>Seatbelt retractors come in various types to ensure maximum safety for vehicle occupants. The Belt-In-Seat design incorporates the seat itself into the seatbelt retraction system. Six-point belts provide additional support by including two extra points of contact. Five-point belts offer a similar level of security with fewer contact points. Four-point belts provide a balance between safety and comfort. Three-point belts are the most common type, offering a secure fit across the lap and shoulder. Two-point belts are less common, mainly found in older vehicles.</p></p>
