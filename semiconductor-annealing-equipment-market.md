<p><ul><li>Lamp-based</li><li>Laser-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor annealing equipment can be categorized into two main types: lamp-based and laser-based. Lamp-based annealing equipment uses intense light from a lamp to heat and process semiconductor materials, while laser-based equipment utilizes a high-powered laser beam for the same purpose. Lamp-based annealing equipment is known for its cost-effectiveness and scalability, making it ideal for large-scale manufacturing. On the other hand, laser-based annealing equipment offers higher precision and control over the annealing process, making it suitable for more specialized applications in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
