<p><p>Single crystal silicon wafers (300mm) market consists of three main types: 300mm epitaxial wafer, 300mm polished wafer, and 300mm annealed wafer. Epitaxial wafers are used for growing thin layers of additional material on the wafer surface. Polished wafers undergo a smoothing process to ensure high quality and precision. Annealed wafers are heat-treated to reduce defects and improve overall quality. Each type serves specific purposes in the semiconductor industry, catering to different manufacturing needs and requirements.</p></p>
