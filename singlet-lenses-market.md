<p><p>Singlet lenses market can be categorized into various types based on their shape and curvature. Plano-convex lenses have one flat surface and one curved surface that bulges outwards. Plano-concave lenses have one flat surface and one inward-curving surface. Biconvex lenses are curved on both sides, creating a thicker middle and thinner edges. Double concave lenses have two inward-curving surfaces. Other types of singlet lenses include meniscus and cylindrical lenses, each with their unique applications and properties.</p></p>
