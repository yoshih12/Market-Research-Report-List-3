<p><p>Smart pigging solutions are commonly used in pipeline inspections for detecting defects and anomalies. The Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL) market type utilizes magnetic sensors to identify areas with metal loss or corrosion. In the Ultrasonic Test (UT) market, high-frequency sound waves are used to locate cracks or flaws in the pipeline. Other market types may include technologies such as eddy current testing or electromagnetic acoustic transducers (EMAT) for comprehensive pipeline inspection and maintenance. These solutions play a crucial role in ensuring the integrity and safety of pipelines.</p></p>
