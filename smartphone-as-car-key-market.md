<p><p>Smartphone as Car Key technology is divided into three main market types: BLE Keys, NFC Keys, and UWB Keys. BLE Keys use Bluetooth Low Energy to establish a connection between the smartphone and the car, allowing for secure unlocking and starting. NFC Keys utilize Near Field Communication for proximity-based communication, making it convenient for users to access and operate their vehicles. UWB Keys, on the other hand, use Ultra-Wideband technology for precise and highly secure communication, offering a higher level of accuracy and reliability in car key operations.</p></p>
