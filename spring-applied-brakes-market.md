<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Spring Applied Brakes</li><li>Electromagnetic Spring Applied Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spring applied brakes can be categorized into two main types: hydraulic spring applied brakes and electromagnetic spring applied brakes. </p><p>Hydraulic spring applied brakes use hydraulic pressure to engage the brake when no power is supplied, making them suitable for heavy-duty machinery where reliability and holding force are essential. </p><p>Electromagnetic spring applied brakes, on the other hand, use electromagnetic force to engage the brake when no power is supplied, offering quick response times and high precision, making them suitable for applications where fast stopping and controlled deceleration are required.</p></p>
