<p><p>The Sputnik Internet of Things market consists of two main types: backhaul services and direct-to-satellite services. Backhaul services provide connectivity for IoT devices by routing data through ground-based networks to satellites, enabling communication over long distances. Direct-to-satellite services bypass ground networks altogether, allowing IoT devices to communicate directly with satellites for faster and more efficient data transmission. Both types of services play a crucial role in expanding connectivity for IoT devices in remote or challenging environments.</p></p>
