<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Styrene acrylic resins are commonly available in two main market types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based resins are environmentally friendly and less toxic, making them suitable for indoor use. They offer low VOC emissions and are easy to clean up with water. On the other hand, solvent-based resins provide better adhesion and durability, making them ideal for outdoor applications and areas with high traffic. However, they typically have higher VOC levels and require solvents for cleanup.</p></p>
