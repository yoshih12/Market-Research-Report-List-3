<p><p>Surgical stapling devices market can be categorized into four main types based on their design and application: straight surgical stapling devices, curved surgical stapling devices, circular surgical stapling devices, and others. Straight surgical stapling devices are used for linear surgical incisions, while curved devices are designed for curved or irregularly shaped tissue. Circular stapling devices are commonly used for creating circular anastomoses in gastrointestinal surgeries. The "others" category includes specialty stapling devices for specific surgical procedures or unique tissue configurations.</p></p>
