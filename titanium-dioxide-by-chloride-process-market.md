<p><p>Titanium dioxide by chloride process market can be categorized into two main types based on crystal structure: rutile and anatase. Rutile titanium dioxide is known for its high refractive index, excellent UV protection, and durability, making it ideal for use in coatings, plastics, and cosmetics. Anatase titanium dioxide, on the other hand, is valued for its high photocatalytic activity, making it suitable for applications in environmental protection and self-cleaning surfaces. Both types of titanium dioxide play crucial roles in various industries due to their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
