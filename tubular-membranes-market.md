<p><p>The Tubular Membranes Market includes two primary types: Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) and Reverse Osmosis (RO) membranes. MBR technology involves using a combination of biological treatment and membrane filtration to treat wastewater. It is commonly used in wastewater treatment plants due to its high efficiency in removing contaminants. On the other hand, RO membranes are used for desalination, removing salts, minerals, and other impurities from water. Both MBR and RO membranes play a crucial role in ensuring clean water supply and environmental protection.</p></p>
