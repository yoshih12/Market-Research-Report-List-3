<p><p>Tuned passive harmonic filters are categorized into Single Tuned Filters, Double Tuned Filters, and Triple Tuned Filters based on the number of tuning branches they have. Single Tuned Filters are designed to eliminate one specific harmonic frequency, while Double Tuned Filters target two specific frequencies. Triple Tuned Filters provide enhanced harmonic suppression by targeting three specific frequencies. Each type of filter offers varying levels of harmonic mitigation, allowing users to choose the best option based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
