<p><p>TWS In-Ear-Monitor market features two primary types of noise cancellation: active and passive. Active noise cancellation technology uses built-in microphones to detect external noise and create sound waves that cancel out these unwanted sounds. This type of noise cancellation is highly effective in blocking out background noise. On the other hand, passive noise canceling relies on the physical design of the earbuds to block out external sounds. Both types of noise cancellation serve to enhance the listening experience by minimizing distractions and improving sound quality.</p></p>
