<p><p>Ultrafiltration (UF) System market can be categorized into Polymeric Ultrafiltration (UF) System and Ceramic Ultrafiltration (UF) System. Polymeric UF systems use synthetic polymeric membranes with varying pore sizes to filter out contaminants from water. On the other hand, Ceramic UF systems utilize inorganic ceramic membranes that are more durable and have higher resistance to chemical and mechanical stress. Both types of UF systems are widely used for water treatment, wastewater purification, and in various industrial processes requiring filtration of liquids.</p></p>
