<p><p>Video Teller Machines (VTMs) come in two main market types: floor-standing type and kiosk type. Floor-standing VTMs are typically larger machines that are freestanding and can easily be integrated into existing bank branches. Kiosk VTMs are smaller, more compact machines that are often placed in more public locations, such as shopping malls or grocery stores. Both types of VTMs offer customers the convenience of conducting various banking transactions remotely with the assistance of a live teller via video chat.</p></p>
