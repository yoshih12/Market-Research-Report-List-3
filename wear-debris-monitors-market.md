<p><p>Wear debris monitors are used to track the amount and size of wear particles in lubricants to assess machinery health. Online wear debris monitors continuously monitor and analyze wear debris in real-time during operation, providing immediate feedback. Offline wear debris monitors require taking samples of the lubricant for analysis in a laboratory setting. While online monitors offer real-time information for proactive maintenance, offline monitors are more suitable for periodic detailed analysis and trending of wear debris over time.</p></p>
