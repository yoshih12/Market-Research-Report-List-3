<p><p>Wired workout headphones come in two main types: back closed-back and back open-back. Closed-back headphones have sealed ear cups that prevent sound leakage and block outside noise. They provide a more immersive listening experience and are ideal for intense workouts. Open-back headphones have ear cups with ventilation holes, allowing air to flow in and out. This design creates a more spacious sound, but also lets in more ambient noise. Open-back headphones are preferred for activities where situational awareness is important.</p></p>
